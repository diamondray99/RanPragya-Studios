Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of your information when you play the game 2048: Infinity.

1. Data Collection and Use

Our game is a free application. To display advertisements, we integrate the Google AdMob SDK. Google AdMob collects certain data from your device to personalize ads, analyze performance, and prevent fraud. The types of data collected may include:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or Google Advertising ID)
  • Coarse location data
  • Ad interaction and performance metrics

2. Cookie Usage and Advertising Preferences

Google AdMob uses cookies and mobile identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can manage your personalized advertising preferences directly inside the game settings under the Privacy Tab or through your Android system settings under Settings > Privacy > Ads.

3. Data Protection and Rights

We comply with all standard store data protection rules. We do not store or transmit any personal player data to our own servers; all data transit is handled directly and securely between your device and the Google AdMob servers.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
